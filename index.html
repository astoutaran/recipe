Flat Eggs

Here's a flat Eggs recipe

Ingredient

  1. 5 Eggs
  2. 1 Tsp vegetable oil
  3. Salt
  4. Black pepper

Cooking time: 3mn

Instructions

Put a pan on low heat, add oil. break eggs in a bowl then add a pinch of salt and pepper. increase the heat to medium, stir with a fork and pour it into the pan. Wait 10 seconds, turn the eggs then turn off the heat.
